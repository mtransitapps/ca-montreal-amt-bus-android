This application adds Montreal RTM (AMT) Express bus information to MonTransit.

This app contains the bus schedule and the latest news from rtm.quebec, YouTube and @allo_exo and @VR_Champlain on Twitter.

RTM (AMT) Express bus serves Montreal and Brossard.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display bus information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain.
https://rtm.quebec/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-montreal-amt-bus-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from rtm.quebec, YouTube and Twitter
